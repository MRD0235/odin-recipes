Bagel with Cream Cheese

Description

This New York City area staple is perfect for an on the go breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bagel
  • 3 Tbsp Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the bagel in half with a knife if it is not precut
  2. Using a knife, spread the cream cheese spread onto each half of the bagel
  3. Enjoy in two pieces of placed together like a sandwich
