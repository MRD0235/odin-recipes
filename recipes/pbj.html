PB&J Sandwich

Description

This sandwich contains peanut butter and jelly between two slices of bread of your choice. It is a quick and easy sandwich to assemble making it perfect for anyone on a time crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 Tbp of peanut butter
  • 2 Tbp of jelly

Steps

  1. Place both slices of bread on a plate
  2. Using a knife, spread the peanut butter onto one slice of bread
  3. Using a spoon, place the jelly on the other slice of bread, and use the knife to spread it evenly
  4. Place both pieces of bread together, and cut the sandwich in half if desired
